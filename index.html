What is Remanufacturing?

Remanufacturing does not mean used! When we remanufacture an alternator or starter we make a detailed assessment of what is and isn’t working in the existing components.

We will then repair or replace the component parts to build a product that’s as good, or in some cases better than what was first sold by the OEM. We back up our work with warranties that meet or exceed those from the first sale of the alternator or starter.
